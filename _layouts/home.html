About

I am currently a postdoc at MIT with Youssef Marzouk, and will start a Juniorprofessorship at the University of Heidelberg in April 2020. Before moving to MIT in 2019, I completed my PhD at ETH Zürich with Christoph Schwab on Sparse-Grid Approximation of High-Dimensional Parametric PDEs. In my free time, I enjoy reading books on history, taking pictures, listening to music and going to museums.

Research

My research focuses on the methodological foundations and theory of forward and inverse problems in Uncertainty Quantification. I am interested in developing and analyzing algorithms for high-dimensional approximation based on sparse-grid techniques, neural networks and transport methods.
